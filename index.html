Career

From humble beginnings to present Day

Mohamad Moussa, MD, FEBU is an associate Professor & Chairman of the Urology Department at the Lebanese University, Beirut, Lebanon. He completed his residency in Urology in Klinikum Fichtelgebirge Hospital, Germany.

He was a consultant urologist and clinical instructor for 4 years in Klinikum Fichtelgebirge hospital. He is currently the head of the Urology Department, Al Zahraa Hospital UMC and the Vice president of the Lebanese Society of Urology.
